About Shahnaz Jasmeen Keya

Shahnaz Jasmeen Keya is a Lecturer in the Department of Education (English) at RTM Al-Kabir Technical University,Sylhet,Bangladesh. She is an educator and researcher with a focus on English Language Teaching and literary studies.
She completed her Bachelor of Arts (B.A. Honours) and Master of Arts (M.A.) from Metropolitan University, Sylhet. She has teaching experience at Al Amanah Academy and Scholarshome South Surma, Sylhet. She has also completed professional training in TESOL Methodology and teacher development training from Arizona State University through the Online Professional English Network (OPEN). Her research interests include Diaspora Studies, Postcolonial Studies, Rabindranath Tagore studies, and English Language Teaching (ELT). She has presented research papers at national conferences and published academic abstracts and creative writings in different platforms, including Spillwords.com. Having published abstracts in different South Asian conferences, she has a fascination for academic research. She has also served as Vice President of the MU Speakers Club of English, showing her involvement in academic leadership and communication activities.
