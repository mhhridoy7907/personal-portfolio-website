About Shahnaz Jasmeen Keya

Shahnaz Jasmeen Keya is currently serving as a Lecturer in the Department of Education at RTM Al-Kabir Technical University (RTM-AKTU).

Profile

She is an academic professional with experience in teaching, research, and communication-based leadership roles.

Education & Training

Ms. Shahnaz Jasmeen Keya has obtained her B.A. (Honours) degree from Metropolitan University, Sylhet.

She has also completed:

  • Teachers Training on TESOL Methodology delivered by University of Maryland, Baltimore County (US Embassy)
  • Course on Professional Development for Teachers Trainer provided by Arizona State University under Online Professional English Network

Previous Work Experience

She has previously worked as:

  • Teacher at Al Amanah Academy
  • Teacher at Scholarshome South Surma, Sylhet
  • Remote Administrator and Call Centre Executive, Community Care Worker, Stroke on Trent

Skills & Activities

She has strong skills in:

  • Communication
  • Analytical thinking
  • Research
  • Management

She also served as Vice President of MU Speakers Club of English.

Research Interests

Her research interests include:

  • Diaspora Studies
  • Postcolonial Studies
  • Repository of Tagore
  • English Language Teaching

She has also presented multiple academic abstracts in South Asian conferences.

Current Position

Lecturer, Department of Education (English)
RTM Al-Kabir Technical University (RTM-AKTU)
